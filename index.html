  • ALPHA Management

    Visit site!

    TL;DR - Establishing an online presence in form of a website and SoMe content for ALPHA Management

    As my exam project for my second semester, I got the honor to establish an online presence for ALPHA management & artist services in form of a website and SoMe material. The website was built on a database with info about their signed artists, events, news and recommendations.

    As their only form of existing visual identity at the time was their logo, which gave us quite free reins in terms of creating their online image. Of course we worked close with ALPHA during the entire process to iteratively change and better the end product.

  • Soup Shenanigans

    TL;DR - Designing game elements in Illustrator, later implementing these into a game using HTML, CSS animations and JavaScript.

    Soup Shenanigans is a creative and simple game that runs using JavaScript and CSS-animations. The goal of the game is to swat the incoming flies away from your soup, while collecting as much garnish as you can to make a delicious dinner. The game elements are designed in Illustrator taking inspiration from flat design.

  • Bastard Café Gin Menu

    TL;DR - Visually designing and collecting information for a gin menu

    In 2022 i got the honor to create and design a gin menu for Bastard Café’s ginbar. I had great fun with it, but have found it to be hard to keep up to date as it is printed out.
